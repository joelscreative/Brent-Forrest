“Dirty Pool”

A timeless tale of good versus not so good.

In 1998, during my final year of high school I got my first animation job at a downtown Toronto studio called Red Rover.I would work there for six years and meet some of the finest animators I’d ever know.

Next door to the studio was a pool hall called the Charlotte Room. There we would spend most of our nights shooting pool, taking the piss out of each other and laughing over beers.

Both the studio and the pool hall are now gone, the studio due to the untimely passing of our boss and mentor Andy, and the pool hall due to condo developers. This film is my personal ode to a time long gone when animation was king and our only worries were keeping our chops fine tuned for league nights.

Dedicated to Andy Knight and the Nine old Men of Rover
