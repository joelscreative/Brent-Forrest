“Dirty Pool”

This film is a quick and personal ode to my animator friends and the pool hall where we used to spend our evenings. The Charlotte Room, is gone forever thanks to unrelenting Toronto Condo developers. It lives on in the hearts and minds of my hometown brothers, and anyone who loved animation and a good pool table. A timeless tale of good versus not so good.
